About Me

HELLO WORLD!! I am thrilled that you are here, visiting my website. I am currently studying Computer Science and Statistics(minor) at Tsinghua University from 2016 to 2020. I was awarded the Global Competence Fellowship for my senior year.

During my first three years of my undergraduate life, I am lucky to have three research experiences in Central America(UIUC), the East Coast(MIT), and the West Coast(Stanford), where I worked on Text Mining, Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning, and Biomedical Informatics. Broadening the horizon of human intelligence and technology development is an honorable mission in my mind. I hope I can explore a challenging new field in the following years.

I got several chances to have a look at a wide range of developing countries. I went to Sri Lanka as a sea turtle protection volunteer. I also studied two weeks at AUC in Egypt and participated in a Deep Dive Program in UAEU in United Arab Emirates. Besides, I have also been to Nepal, Singapore, and Turkey. The most unbelievable experience is participating in the BRICS Youth Forum, a highest-level official youth diplomacy event, as a delegate.
